Official Terms & Conditions

Please read our operational terms to ensure a smooth shifting process:

  • We do not undertake Electrical, Carpentry & Plumber Jobs.
  • Vehicle transportation charges are based on present prevailing market rates.
  • We or our agent shall be exempted from any kind of loss or damage done due to accident, pilferage, fire, rain collision, or any road/riot hazard. To avoid loss, we strongly advise insuring your consignment.
  • We request complete payment at the loading point along with your purchase order before departure.
  • Insurance premium must be paid at the loading point before departure.
  • If required, we provide secure storage facilities at a nominal charge.
  • Cars being transported should have at least 10 Liters of Petrol. Extra payment will be charged if specialized Wooden Packing is requested on moving date.
  • We are not responsible for Gold & Cash. Please keep valuables in your custody.
  • PROHIBITED ITEMS: Carrying Liquor, Gas Cylinders, Acids, or liquids (like oil/ghee tins) is strictly prohibited.
